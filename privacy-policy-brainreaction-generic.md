Depending on the specific Application, the Service Provider may collect the following categories of information:

- Basic device information such as operating system version, used to ensure correct functioning of the Application.
- Usage data such as screens or features accessed and time spent in the Application, collected in aggregate and anonymised form.

The Service Provider does **not** use Artificial Intelligence technologies to process your personal data.
